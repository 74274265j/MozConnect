Keynote Speakers

Sayak Sarkar

RedHat Software Developer, Ex-Council &Mozilla Reps Mentor

Sayak Sarkar is a Mozilla Reps Mentor, Webmaker Super Mentor and Community Builder. He has been an Intern at Mozilla Foundation. Along side his contributions to Mozilla, Sayak is a contributor to other FOSS projects like Mozilla, Mediawiki, GNOME, Cryptocat, Fedora and WordPress in varying capacities from bn_IN l10n and bug reporting to web dev and bug fixing. He is currently working Associate Application Developer at Red Hat.

Soumya Deb

Lead Frontend and UX "Debloper" at Red Hat

Mr. Soumya Deb is a post graduate in Computer Applications from Siliguri Institute of Technology. Starting his career as a Junior Writer at 9 Dot 9 Media Pvt. Ltd., he wrote about high rated-featured articles about Internet & web-technology, multimedia, tech-politics, smarter-mobility etc. Having worked with various companies and contributor to open-source projects, currently, he is working as Lead Frontend Developer at Red Hat. He's also a Mozilla Reps Mentor and a featured Mentor for April 2012.

@Debloper @Debloper

Chandrakant Dhutadmal

Senior Technical Officer, C-DAC

Mr. Chandrakant Dhutadmal is a post graduate in computer engineering from university of Pune. Starting his career as a University of Pune approved faculty in computer engineering stream, he served various engineering colleges for a period of five years before joining Centre for development of advanced computing (C-DAC) in January, 2006 as Member Technical Staff. Currently, he is working as Senior Technical officer, in Graphics and Intelligence based scripting technology (GIST) group of C-DAC. His specialization includes Localization and Internationalization, Project Management, Software and Quality Engineering and is currently working on various research projects of State and National importance. He is a Certified Localization Professional (CLP Level 1) from The Institute of Localization Professional (TILP) since 2010 and Distinction holder in online certification course in eGov of Govt. of Maharashtra. He is also active member of free and open source projects like “Mozilla Firefox” and “FUEL” project.

Sara Khan

Sara Khan is a Mozilla Reps Mentor and Software Developer by profession at DB Xento Systems. She is leading Womoz Community in India. She is also helping with Mozilla L10n projects for Urdu locale.
